Coding is the process of writing instructions for a computer to follow using programming languages such as C, Python, or Java. It is often called the “language of computers” because it tells machines how to perform specific tasks, from simple calculations to running complex applications. Coding is used to create software, websites, mobile apps, and even control hardware like robots or smart devices. It helps in solving problems logically and efficiently while improving creativity and critical thinking. In today’s digital world, coding has become an essential skill, opening doors to innovation, automation, and career opportunities in technology.
