Flowers are the most beautiful and important part of a plant, mainly responsible for reproduction.
They are the reproductive structures of flowering plants (angiosperms) and usually consist of four main parts:
sepals, petals, stamens, and carpels.
The bright colors and fragrance of flowers attract insects, birds, and other pollinators, which help in transferring pollen from one flower to another.< br> This process of pollination leads to fertilization, resulting in the formation of fruits and seeds.
